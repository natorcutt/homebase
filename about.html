About SmartLens

Do you ever feel like you are missing out on life because of the need to check your phone? Do you ever feel nervous or worried that you might be missing something important, or forget to do something because you are distracted by the hustle and bustle of your busy life?

These are some of the main ideas behind SmartLens. Life moves pretty fast and with technology evolving and becoming so intertwined in our lives, it is hard to stay focused and not feel like you are missing or forgetting something.

That's where we come in.

SmartLens glasses will give you the freedom and indepence you want without the nagging feeling of checking your phone all the time to stay up to date with your life. With two innovative modes: Update Mode and SmartSight Mode, constantly checking your phone or taking in your surroundings without lifting a finger. All you need is a bluetooth connection and you can instantly recieve live and realtime updates and notifcations from your phone- as well as what is going on around you allowing you to multi-task and make the most out of your valueable time and life for things that really matter. All in a simplistic and stylish way that is true to the real you.

How it Works:

Using cutting edge technology, SmartSight will free the viewer from distractions and allow them to either focus more on what is going on around them, or focus more on the ask at hand while maintaining awareness of their surroundings.

Using the headphone ear bud attached to each pair of SmartLens glasses, the viewer can turn their SmartLens glasses onto SmartSight mode, and the SmartLens Assistant will call out important objects in the viewer’s surroundings, alert them to potential dangers, things they might run into if they are distracted, and give directions.

The SmartSight feature is also the first set of Smart glasses that are proven to assist in Accessibility and mobility for customers with visual impairments, specifically for low vision customers. The ability of SmartLens to narrate, point out possible obstacles and explain the viewer’s surroundings, and give directions significantly improve the customer’s independence and freedom to move around a specific area on their own.

Through enhanced Bluetooth connectivity, SmartLens allows you to preset which mobile applications and accounts you would like to recieve push notifications from on your SmartLens lenses.

By allowing you personalize what notifications you see and which are of importance to you, SmartLens allows you to simplify your life, while always staying up to date and in the know- without ever having to check your phone screen again.

Update Mode also gives you the ability to filter which notifications appear on which lens, offering you an additional layer of personalization and customization.
