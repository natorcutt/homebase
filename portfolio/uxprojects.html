Experience Architecture Website Editing

In a short summer internship at the College of Arts and Letters I was given the task of reworking the content and set up of the new Experience Architecture website. I changed and edited all of the text content on each page, adding new pages when I saw fit and rewriting much of the content so that it would appeal to the audiences of prospective students, current students, potential employers and faculty. I learned a lot about operating in Concrete5, as well as writing and simplifying complicated concepts so that it can be understood by people not necessarily acquainted with a certain field of study.
